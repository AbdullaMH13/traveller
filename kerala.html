Welcome to Kerala

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country", is a tropical paradise on the southwestern coast of India. Famous for its backwaters, beaches, hill stations, and rich culture, Kerala offers an unforgettable experience.

Top Tourist Destinations in Kerala

Alleppey

Famous for its tranquil backwaters and houseboats, Alleppey is a must-visit for nature lovers.

Kochi

Kochi, also known as Cochin, is a vibrant city rich in culture, with its historic architecture, beautiful backwaters, and scenic surroundings. It’s a perfect blend of old and new.

Munnar

A scenic hill station known for its lush tea gardens and beautiful mountain views.

Kovalam

One of Kerala's most famous beaches, perfect for sunbathing and water sports.

Varkala

Varkala is known for its stunning cliffs, scenic beaches, and the Papanasam Beach which is considered sacred. It’s a perfect blend of spiritual, natural, and beach tourism.

Kozhikode

Kozhikode, also known as Calicut, is a historic city famous for its beautiful beaches, rich cultural heritage, and iconic landmarks like the Kozhikode Beach and the Mananchira Square. It is known for its delicious Malabar cuisine, including the famous Kozhikode biryani. The city offers a perfect mix of history, nature, and culinary delights.
