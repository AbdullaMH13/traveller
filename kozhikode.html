Kozhikode Travel Itinerary

Discover the Historical Charm of Calicut

Kozhikode: A Perfect Blend of Heritage and Nature

Kozhikode (Calicut) is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and beautiful beaches. From historic landmarks to scenic spots, this city is a perfect blend of modernity and tradition.

Day 1: Historical Exploration

  • Morning: Kappad Beach - Visit the historic Kappad Beach where Vasco da Gama landed in 1498.
  • Afternoon: Kozhikode Beach - Take a stroll on Kozhikode Beach, relax, and enjoy local snacks.
  • Evening: Mananchira Square - Explore this heritage site with a beautiful pond and surroundings.

Day 2: Culture and Adventure

  • Morning: Pazhassi Raja Museum - Discover the history of Kerala through its artifacts.
  • Afternoon: Tali Temple - Visit this ancient temple known for its intricate architecture.
  • Evening: Backwater Ride - End your day with a peaceful boat ride along Kozhikode's backwaters.

Additional Activities

  • Sree Muthappan Temple - A beautiful temple known for its vibrant rituals.
  • Calicut University Campus - Explore the sprawling campus surrounded by nature.

Food Spots in Kozhikode

  • Paragon Restaurant - Famous for its Malabar Biryani and delicious local dishes.
  • Malayala Manorama - A must-visit for authentic Kerala food and snacks.
  • Sweetmeat Street - Try Kozhikode's famous Halwa and other sweet treats.

Adventure Activities in Kozhikode

  • Kayaking - Explore the backwaters of Kozhikode in a kayak.
  • Hiking in Wayanad - Take a short trip to Wayanad and enjoy hiking in the Western Ghats.
  • Camping - Experience the wilderness and camp under the stars in the serene nature spots around Kozhikode.

Enjoy your trip to Kozhikode and experience the beautiful fusion of nature, history, and culture!
