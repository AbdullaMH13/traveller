Munnar Travel Itinerary

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Kerala's most famous hill station

Your Ultimate Munnar Experience

Nestled amidst the Western Ghats at an altitude of 6,000 ft, Munnar is a paradise of rolling hills, misty valleys, and endless tea plantations. Once a summer resort of the British government, this hill station boasts stunning landscapes, diverse flora and fauna, and a pleasant climate throughout the year.

Our carefully curated itinerary helps you discover the best of Munnar's natural beauty, cultural heritage, and culinary delights.

Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit

    September to May for pleasant weather

  • Getting There

    Nearest airport: Cochin International (105 km)

    Nearest railway station: Aluva (110 km)

  • Local Transport

    Taxis and auto-rickshaws available for local travel

    Consider renting a car for flexibility

  • Weather

    Pack light woolens even in summer as evenings can be cool

    Carry rain gear during monsoon (June-August)

Road conditions can be winding - take motion sickness medication if needed.
