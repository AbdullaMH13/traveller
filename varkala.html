Varkala Travel Itinerary

Explore the Hidden Gem of Kerala

Varkala: A Coastal Paradise

Varkala is an amazing beach town in Kerala, offering a tranquil and scenic environment with pristine beaches, temples, and natural beauty. It's a perfect destination for both relaxation and adventure.

Day 1: Beach Time and Temple Visit

  • Morning: Varkala Beach - Start your day relaxing on the pristine sands of Varkala Beach.
  • Afternoon: Janardhana Swamy Temple - Visit the ancient temple, dedicated to Lord Vishnu, known for its serene atmosphere.
  • Evening: Sunset at Papanasam Beach - End your day by witnessing the stunning sunset at Papanasam Beach.

Day 2: Adventure and Culture

  • Morning: Paragliding - Take a thrilling paragliding ride over the beautiful coastline of Varkala.
  • Afternoon: Varkala Cliff - Explore the famous Varkala Cliff, a great spot for sightseeing and photography.
  • Evening: Relaxing Ayurvedic Treatment - Unwind with an Ayurvedic massage at a local spa.
